About Bridget

Hello! I'm Bridget and I'm a aspiring web developer and designer. My professional past varies from advertising, fashion design, and sales but I've found that all aspects of the present day workplace revoles around technology and design. I've always had a passion for creating, starting to draw from an early age, and that has become a passion of mine. I have an Etsy boutique where I sell original painting and drawings at any request. I love to design nursery art and create unique gifts by drawing houses that familes can cherish.


I hope to translate my work to the digital landscape by learning how to develop and design websites so welcome!
